## Troubleshooting

### I sent `hi` but got no welcome message

1. Check internet and WhatsApp is working.
2. Make sure you messaged the **correct** NewsDash number.
3. Wait 2–3 minutes and try sending `hi` again.
4. Contact support with your phone number.

### I am not getting scheduled news

1. Confirm you completed **Step 2** (sent `hi`).
2. News is sent on a schedule — you may not get a message every hour.
3. Contact support to verify your number is active.

### I asked a question but got no answer

1. Use a clear topic (see Step 5).
2. Wait up to 2 minutes.
3. Try again with `/ask your topic`.
4. Contact support if it still fails.

### I want to stop messages

Send **STOP** in the WhatsApp chat, or ask the NewsDash team to remove your number.
